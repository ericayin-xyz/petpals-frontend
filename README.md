# PetPals - Full Stack App
### Developed by Erica (Luyue)

Visit our website at: https://mellifluous-dieff.netlify.app

Welcome to PetPals, your go-to destination for connecting with dedicated pet care providers and discovering opportunities in pet care.  Our platform effortlessly bridges the gap between pet owners in need of reliable care for their pets and individuals seeking rewarding job opportunities as pet sitters.  Whether you're planning a vacation, need someone to accompany your dog on daily walks, require in-home pet care, or are looking to embark on a fulfilling career in pet sitting, PetPals has you covered.  We're committed to ensuring that every pet receives the love and care they deserve, while also empowering pet lovers to turn their passion into a profession.

## Purpose
PetPals aims to provide pet owners with a seamless and flexible solution for pet care, connecting them with trustworthy individuals passionate about animals. This platform serves not only as a convenient option for pet care but also as a community where pet lovers can offer and seek support.

## Target Audience

- **Pet Owners**: Those in need of reliable pet care services.
- **Pet Sitters**: Individuals looking to offer pet care services, from students seeking casual work to pet enthusiasts eager to connect with other pet lovers.

    ### For Pet Parents
    - **Search for Pet Sitters**: Easily find pet care based on specific needs, such as location.
    - **Book Appointments**: Directly contact pet sitters to schedule appointments, simplifying the process of arranging the care your pet needs.
    - **Reviews and Ratings**: Share and read experiences to ensure quality care.


    ### For Pet Sitters
    - **Create Sitter Profiles**: For those looking to become pet sitters, creating profiles that showcase your location and experience will allow pet owners to get to know you better before booking.

## Tech Stack

- **Front-end**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React
- **Back-end**: JavaScript, Node.js, Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Project Management**: Trello
- **Source Control**: Git, GitHub
- **Wireframes**: Balsamiq

## Getting Started

In the project directory, you can run:

- `npm start`: 
Runs the app in development mode. Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) to view it in your browser.

    The page will reload when you make changes.
You may also see any lint errors in the console.

- `npm test`
Launches the test runner in the interactive watch mode.

    See the section about running tests for more information.

- `npm run build`
Builds the app for production to the build folder.
It correctly bundles React in production mode and optimizes the build for the best performance.

    The build is minified and the filenames include the hashes.
Your app is ready to be deployed!
